How to use a Clip On Tie?

While clip-on ties are practical, they can sometimes be perceived as less formal than their tied counterparts. However, when chosen thoughtfully in quality fabrics and stylish designs, they can still make a refined statement. In summary, a clip-on tie is a versatile and convenient accessory that combines practicality with style. Whether for everyday wear, special occasions, or professional environments, it offers an effortless solution for those seeking the polished look of a tie without the complexity of tying one.

How to use a Clip On Tie

Instructions:
  • Select a collared shirt to wear with your clip-on tie. Ensure the collar is clean and crisp for the best appearance.
  • Hold the clip-on tie by its narrow end (the tail) and locate the clip at the back of the wider end (the blade). The clip should be facing upwards.
  • Drape the clip-on tie around your neck with the wider end hanging down over the front of your shirt. The narrow end should be positioned behind the wider end.
  • Align the wider end of the tie so that it sits just above the collar of your shirt, centered in the middle. Ensure that the tie is positioned comfortably against your neck.
  • Take the clip at the back of the wider end of the tie and carefully clip it to the underside of the collar. Make sure it secures firmly but without pulling too tightly, as it should still allow for comfort.

  • Smooth out any wrinkles in the tie and ensure that it lays flat against your shirt. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the clip is secure and the tie looks neat.
